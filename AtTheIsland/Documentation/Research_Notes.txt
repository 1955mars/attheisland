zSpace:

zSpace is a technology firm based in Sunnyvale, California that combine elements of virtual and augmented reality in a computer.
zSpace allows people to interact with simulated objects in virtual environments as if they are real. 
zSpace is known for its progressive developments in human-computer interaction.

EON Reality is a multinational virtual reality and augmented reality software developer headquartered in Irvine, California.
It provides simulation based solutions for various industries like Education, Medical & Health, Manufacturing & Industrial, Aerospace & Transportation. 

Veative:


Veative is a global provider of education technology and learning simulations for schools and industries.
It uses immersive technologies such as 3D, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality.
Veative's focus is to bring significant educational transformation by introducing immersive and interactive modules within classrooms.



ImmersiveVrEducation:

VREducation is a VR/AR software firm dedicated to changing how educational and corporate training are provided and consumed globally.
It develops tools for educators and corporate trainers to create their own content using virtual classrooms or environments.
It's vision for the future is to see VR and AR become a staple tool for education and corporate training globally.

Labster:
